Safety Relief Valves

Comprehensive inspection, repair, and industry-leading on-line testing solutions.

Control Valve Services Ltd. provide a comprehensive ‘turnkey’ service for the inspection, test, overhaul and repair of Safety Relief valves. Our service is professional, fast and efficient and is carried out by competent, qualified technicians across our bespoke repair centres in Brighouse and Middlesbrough.

Inspection & Refurbishment

Our standard refurbishment process ensures your critical safety assets are returned to OEM standards:

  • Pre-test & Evaluation
  • Full Dis-assembly & Internal Cleaning
  • Detailed Inspection & Documentation
  • Seat Skimming (when required)
  • Disc & Seat Precision Lapping
  • Re-assembly & Pressure Testing
  • Final Re-certification, Sealing & Tagging

Teson II On-Line Testing

Our Teson II system delivers safe and accurate on-line safety relief valve testing – without operational disruption to your plant.

  • No plant downtime required
  • TÜV & DNV approved system
  • Minimal loss of plant pressure/medium
  • Digital management of setting & test results
  • Execution at actual site operating conditions
  • Detailed analysis & electronic reporting

Maintain Productivity & Safety

Teson II identifies valves in need of adjustment or full service while they remain in-situ. This allows correctly performing valves to remain in service for longer periods, significantly reducing the time required to commission plant after shutdown.
