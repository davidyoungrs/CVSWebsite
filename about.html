Engineering Resilience

"Beyond Repair: Delivering Lifecycle Asset Integrity."

Control Valve Services was established in 2007 and stands as the U.K.’s premier independent authority on control valve lifecycle management. Founded on a bedrock of precision engineering, we have evolved into a strategic partner for the world’s most demanding industrial sectors—including Power Generation, Petrochemical, and Renewable Energy.

Operating from our purpose-built, state-of-the-art facility in West Yorkshire, we bridge the gap between OEM quality and independent agility. Our mission is simple: to eliminate unplanned downtime through data-driven diagnostics and world-class mechanical overhaul.

Technical Sovereignty

"We are OEM-agnostic. Our engineers possess the deep-tier expertise to service, calibrate, and certify valves from every major global manufacturer, ensuring your plant remains standardized and compliant."

Diagnostic Intelligence

"We don't just fix symptoms; we solve root causes. Utilizing advanced Acoustic Emissions testing and digital signature analysis, we provide a transparent window into your valve’s performance without interrupting your flow."

National Response
