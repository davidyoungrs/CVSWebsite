About Control Valve Services

Leading the industry with innovative technologies and a pioneering approach to predictive maintenance.

Control Valve Services was established in October 2007 to provide an independent specialist control valve maintenance service to our clients. Our aim was (and still is) to provide a credible and competitive alternative to using OEM support; paying sometimes extortionate OEM prices for a less than adequate level of service.

Control Valve Services has built a solid business, working with international blue-chip clients who appreciate our dedication, attention to detail and ability to deliver on our promises. We lead the way utilising innovative technologies and constantly pushing the boundaries within the control valve industry.
