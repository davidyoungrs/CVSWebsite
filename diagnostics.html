Control Valve Diagnostics

UK Market Leaders in condition monitoring and predictive maintenance technology.

CVS were first to market in the UK with control valve diagnostics technology. Adopting control valve condition monitoring as part of your maintenance regime offers significant financial and environmental benefits. By ensuring your control valves are calibrated to a laboratory standard, we optimise performance to improve plant efficiency and reliability.

Our systems monitor both the mechanical condition of the valve and the instrument condition of actuators, positioners, and associated instrumentation, as well as identifying costly pneumatic leakages. This full-spectrum measurement provides a comprehensive picture of your control valve assets' condition.
