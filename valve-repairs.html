Control Valve Repairs

Bespoke repair and overhaul services for mission-critical infrastructure.

Control Valve Services Ltd. Operate two bespoke fully equipped 10,000sqft control valve repair centres in Brighouse, West Yorkshire and Middlesbrough, Teesside. This allows CVS to support our clients performing all aspects of work relating to control valve inspection, repair / overhaul, refurbishment works and upgrades.

Turnkey Repair Solutions

Our facility is able to perform a total turnkey solution relating to control valve repair / overhaul including:

  • Control Valve Strip Down, Inspection and electronic reporting
  • Emergency quick turnaround service for control valve problems
  • Specialist Portable Control Valve Diagnostics
  • Outage support / high volume conventional control valve overhaul
  • Control Valve Problem, Diagnosis & rectification service
  • Extensive control valve body & control valve trim repairs
  • Refurbishment, including shot blasting and painting to client specification
  • Control Valve Actuation and Instrumentation complete overhaul / refurbishment & upgrade service
  • Emergency control valve machining support for all control valve trim components
  • Emergency alternative control valve trim manufacture
  • Emergency control valve trim supply including material sourcing and manufacture
  • Control valve components heat treatment / material hardening service
  • Control valve trim Stellite #6 Overlay service and finish machining
  • Control valve trim Dense Chrome Plating service and finish machining
  • Control valve trim Pre-Machine / Weld overlay service and finish machining
  • Control valve trim Metal Spray coating & HVOF Coating (High Velocity Oxygen Fuel)
  • Control valve trim Precision Grinding
  • Safety Relief Valve inspection, repair and re-certification
